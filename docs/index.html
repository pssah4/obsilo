What you can do with Obsilo

A few things people use it for every day.

Ask your vault

Ask "What do I know about project X?" and get a synthesized answer drawn from across your notes — no keyword searching required.

Create and edit notes

Generate meeting notes, weekly reviews, and templates. Edit content across multiple files in one task — with a diff preview before anything changes.

Research and save

Tell the agent to research a topic on the web, summarize what it finds, and save the result directly to your vault.

Organize in bulk

Tag notes by pattern, update frontmatter across a folder, move files, or run multi-step cleanup workflows — all with your approval at each step.

Your vault stays on your device

Obsilo runs entirely inside Obsidian. Only the text you send as a prompt is shared with your chosen AI provider. No cloud sync, no telemetry, no account required.
