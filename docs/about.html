About me

I'm Sebastian, a product and technology generalist based in Germany. I've spent my career at the intersection of strategy, product development, and applied technology — working with early-stage projects and established teams alike to build things that actually matter.

My interest in knowledge work, thinking tools, and AI led me to Obsidian — and eventually to building Obsilo Agent: an autonomous AI operating layer for your vault that respects your privacy, runs on local or remote models of your choice, and stays fully open source.

Why Obsilo Agent

I wanted an AI that works inside my knowledge system rather than alongside it. One that understands my notes, follows my workflows, and can actually do things — not just answer questions. Working with Kilo Code in VS Code inspired me what agentic loops should look like when operating inside another tool. Obsilo Agent is that idea applied to knowledge work.
